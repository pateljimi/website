Winter treks

    1. Brahmatal trek

    At 12,600 feet, you will find yourself reaching for those peaks with your hand because that's how close they appear from the Brahmatal Summit. You get a chance to camp along the dazzling glacial lake under a million stars. What else could you wish for?

  • Region: Lohajung, Uttarakhand

    • 2. Chadar Trek

    Need I say anything about the most sought-after trek in Leh? Walking on frozen ice bed is fun and thrilling. The captivating views of the Nerak Falls and the beautiful river flowing under the sheet of ice are a perfect mixture of serenity and adventure.

  • Region: Leh, Ladakh

    • 3. Goecha La Trek

    Trekking in Sikkim and that too in winters is an altogether different experience than trekking in North. The trial of the Goecha La trek is one of it's kind. From the lush green forests to the snow-laden campsites, this trek gives you a chance to experience nature at its best. The varies flora and fauna on this trek are also eye-catching.

  • Region: Yuksom, Sikkim

    • 4. Kedarkantha Trek

    The 'Queens of Winter Treks', Kedarkantha is a popular choice for the trekkers and beginners. The perfect campsites and the mesmerizing views along with the experience of the summit is something one only finds on the trail of Kedarkantha. This trek is the perfect choice for those who want to venture into the world of winter hiking.

  • Region: Sankri, Uttarakhand

    • 5. Kuari Pass Trek

    Kuari Pass means the doorway to heaven. And boy, does it live up to its name? The trail of the trek is so beautiful that you'd be in no hurry to reach the top. The beautiful snow-laden peaks visible from the trail along the amazing flora and fauna make the trek almost perfect.

  • Region: Joshimath, Uttrakhand

    • 6. Har ki Dun

    Har ki Dun s a paradise for bird watchers and nature lovers. The valley is surrounded by dense Pine forests and offers views of glittering mountain peaks. Not just the trail, the campsites of this trek are also very pretty. You will find yourself a beautiful and quiet place.

  • Region: Sankri, Uttarakhand

    • 7. Nag Tibba Winter Trek

    Nearest trek to Delhi. Fit for kids and beginners. Short and budget-friendly. This trek is ideal for all those who do not have many ties on hands and want a complete experience of a trek. The trail has lush green surroundings and snow too. Need I say more?

  • Region: Pantwari, Uttarakhand

    • 8. Panwali Kantha Trek

    One of the most beautiful treks, Panwali Kantha Trek provides you with one of the rarest views of the complete range of Yamnotri- Gangotri- Kedarnath- Badrinath mountain peaks. Along with these mesmerizing views, you get to cross seven bugyals or meadows and if you are lucky enough, you might happen to come across the rare blue mountain goat.

  • Region: Dehradun, Uttarakhand

    • 9. Dayara Bugyal Trek

    Dayara Bugyal is perhaps one of the most beautiful alpine meadows in India. The meadow stretches out far and wide. The panoramic view of the Himalayas from here is breathtaking. Situated at an elevation of about 3750 m, this vast meadow is second to none in natural beauty. The trek goes through open stretches and dense forest at different places. In winters the meadows are covered with snow & look extremely beautiful.

  • Region: Uttarkashi, Uttarakhand

    • 10. Pangarchulla Peak Trek

    The Pangarchulla Peak is a mighty peak in the Uttarakhand at 14,700 ft which is no small feat to conquer. Giving you stunning views of Mt. Nanda Devi, Mt. Dronagiri, Mt. Garud and many more astonishing visuals all throughout the trail, this trek will test your endurance and push your limits. This trek is perfect for those looking to move up a notch with some of the most stunning trekkable peaks of the Himalayas.

  • Region: Joshimath, Uttarakhand
